Pakistan

About

Pakistan is a country in South Asia, created in 1947. Its capital city is Islamabad, and its largest city is Karachi. Pakistan is known for its rich culture, diverse landscapes, and historical places. The country has mountains, rivers, deserts, and beautiful valleys. Urdu is the national language, and Islam is the main religion. Pakistan is also famous for its hospitality and traditional food.
